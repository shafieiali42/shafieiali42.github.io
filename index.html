Publications

Emo3D: Metric and Benchmarking Dataset for 3D Facial Expression Generation from Emotion Description

Dehghani, M., Shafiee* , A., Shafiei * , A., Fallah, N., Alizadeh, F., Gholinejad, M.M., Behroozi, H., Habibi, J., & Asgari, E.

Arxiv Link.

TLDR; The paper introduces "Emo3D," a dataset of text, images, and 3D facial expressions for a wide range of emotions. It leverages language models and vision-language models (like CLIP) for 3D expression synthesis and proposes a new evaluation metric, Emo3D, which outperforms traditional metrics. Applications include animation, virtual reality, and emotional AI.
